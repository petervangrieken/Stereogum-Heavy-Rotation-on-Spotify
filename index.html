Heavy Rotation

Stereogums guide to the world of hipster music

Stereogums Heavy Rotation section on the website is great. What isn't great; there's no RSS or Spotify playlist to search for these releases. To keep up-to-date with the freshest releases one needs to check out the site and search for the releases manually. Since we're living in 2012 (where's my jetpack?!) I made this little app. It updates itself automatically with the newest releases in Heavy Rotation.

Made with love by @janhoogeveen for Usevine in 2012.

This application is in no way endorsed or supported by Stereogum. This is a fan-made project.

My sincere thanks go out to Peter Watts for releasing the Spotify KitchenSink, thereby making it way more easy to develop new applications for Spotify.
